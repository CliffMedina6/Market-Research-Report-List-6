<p><p>Distance health technologies, such as telemedicine and remote patient monitoring, have two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise solutions require the software and hardware to be installed and maintained on the healthcare provider's premises, offering greater control and security but requiring higher upfront costs. Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering more flexibility and scalability with lower initial investment.
