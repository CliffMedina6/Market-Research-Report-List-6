<p><p>Agricultural biological growth stimulants can be categorized into two main types: natural and biosynthetic. Natural growth stimulants are derived from naturally occurring sources such as plant extracts, animal by-products, and beneficial microorganisms. These products are considered organic and sustainable. On the other hand, biosynthetic growth stimulants are chemically synthesized compounds that mimic the effects of natural growth promoters. While they are not considered organic, they are often more cost-effective and can be tailored for specific agricultural needs.</p></p>
