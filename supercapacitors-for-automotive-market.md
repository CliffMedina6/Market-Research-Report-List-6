<p><p>The automotive market for supercapacitors is divided into two main types: energy capacitors and power capacitors. Energy capacitors store and deliver energy gradually, making them ideal for applications requiring sustained energy supply, such as regenerative braking systems. In contrast, power capacitors release energy rapidly, catering to high-demand scenarios like acceleration and power surge management. Both types enhance vehicle efficiency, reduce emissions, and support advancements in electric and hybrid technologies, contributing to the overall development of sustainable automotive solutions.</p></p>
