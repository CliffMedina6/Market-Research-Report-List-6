<p><p>Sun-screening agents can be classified into different types based on their active ingredients. Zinc oxide type sunscreens provide broad-spectrum protection and are effective in blocking both UVA and UVB rays. Titanium dioxide type sunscreens work by reflecting and scattering UV radiation. Octyl salicylate type sunscreens are organic compounds that absorb UV radiation. Other types of sunscreens may include avobenzone, oxybenzone, and octocrylene, each offering unique benefits and characteristics for sun protection. Consumers can choose from a variety of sunscreens based on their preferences and skin needs.</p></p>
