<p><p>Low pressure vacuum carburizing furnaces are available in three main types: Single Chamber, Double Chamber, and Multi Chamber. Single Chamber furnaces are designed for smaller batch sizes and are ideal for job shops or small-scale production. Double Chamber furnaces have two separate chambers for loading and carburizing, allowing for increased throughput and efficiency. Multi Chamber furnaces have multiple chambers operating simultaneously, allowing for even higher production volumes. Each type of furnace offers unique advantages depending on the needs of the user.</p></p>
