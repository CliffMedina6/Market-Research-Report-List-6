<p><p>Orifice fittings are used in fluid flow measurement applications. Dual chamber orifice fittings have two separate chambers for fluid flow measurement, offering higher accuracy and reliability. Single chamber orifice fittings have only one chamber for fluid flow measurement, making them more compact and cost-effective. Both types are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and water treatment. The choice between dual chamber and single chamber orifice fittings depends on the specific requirements of the application in terms of accuracy, cost, and space constraints.</p></p>
