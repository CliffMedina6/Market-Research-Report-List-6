<p><p>Diabetes lancing devices come in two main types: adjustable and non-adjustable. Adjustable lancing devices allow users to select the depth of the lancet insertion, providing more control and customization for individual comfort levels. On the other hand, non-adjustable lancing devices have a fixed depth setting, making them simpler to use but offering less flexibility. Both types serve the same purpose of obtaining a blood sample for glucose monitoring, but differ in terms of user control and customization options.</p></p>
