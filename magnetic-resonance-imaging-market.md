<p><p>Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) market is divided into two main types: Closed MRI Systems and Open MRI Systems. Closed MRI Systems are traditional machines with a tunnel-like structure, providing high-quality images but may cause discomfort to patients who experience claustrophobia. Open MRI Systems offer a more spacious and less confining environment, suitable for patients who may feel anxious in closed systems. Both types cater to different patient needs and preferences, contributing to the diverse options available in the MRI market.</p></p>
