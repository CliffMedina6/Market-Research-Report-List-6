<p><strong>The Drum Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD scanner</li><li>PMT sanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum scanners can be categorized into two main types: CCD scanners and PMT scanners. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) scanners utilize solid-state technology for high-speed and efficient image capture, offering excellent color accuracy and resolution, making them popular in commercial applications. PMT (PhotoMultiplier Tube) scanners, on the other hand, use vacuum tubes to detect light, providing superior sensitivity and dynamic range, which is beneficial for archival purposes. Both types serve distinct needs in the imaging and graphic arts industries.</p></p>
