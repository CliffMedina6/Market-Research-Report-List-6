<p><p>The Secured Socket Layer (SSL) Certification market comprises three main types: Domain Validated (DV) SSL Certificates, Organization Validated (OV) SSL Certificates, and Extended Validation (EV) SSL Certificates. DV SSLs provide basic encryption and are validated through domain ownership, making them quick and easy to acquire. OV SSLs require verification of the organization's identity, offering additional trust. EV SSLs provide the highest level of validation, requiring extensive verification and displaying the organization’s name prominently in the browser, enhancing credibility.</p></p>
