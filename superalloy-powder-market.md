Iron Based Superalloys are known for their high strength and toughness, making them ideal for applications in aerospace and automotive industries. Cobalt Based Superalloys are preferred for their excellent high-temperature strength and corrosion resistance. Nickel Based Superalloys are widely used in gas turbine engines and chemical processing due to their superior creep resistance and thermal stability. Each type offers unique properties suited for specific industry needs.</p></p>
