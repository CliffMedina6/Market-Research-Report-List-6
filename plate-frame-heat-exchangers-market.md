<p><p>Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers are commonly categorized into three types: Brazed, Gasketed, and Welded. Brazed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers are compact and efficient, utilizing brazed joints for increased durability. Gasketed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers use gaskets to seal plates together, allowing for easier maintenance and repair. Welded Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers are known for their high pressure and temperature capabilities due to the welded construction. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
