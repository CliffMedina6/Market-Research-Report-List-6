<p><p>Medical suture market is divided into two main types: absorbable suture and non-absorbable suture. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down naturally in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal after the wound has healed. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are intended to remain in the body indefinitely and may need to be removed by a healthcare professional. Both types of sutures are widely used in various surgical procedures to close wounds and promote healing.</p></p>
