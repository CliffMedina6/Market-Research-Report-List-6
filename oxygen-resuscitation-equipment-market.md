<p><p>Oxygen Resuscitation Equipment Market includes two main types: Self-inflating Oxygen Resuscitation Equipment and Flow-inflating Oxygen Resuscitation Equipment. Self-inflating equipment automatically inflates with oxygen when squeezed, making it easy to use in emergency situations. Flow-inflating equipment requires a separate oxygen source to inflate, providing more control over oxygen delivery. Both types are essential for providing ventilatory support to patients in respiratory distress and are commonly used in medical settings such as hospitals, ambulances, and emergency rooms.</p></p>
