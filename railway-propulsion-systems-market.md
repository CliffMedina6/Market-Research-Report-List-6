<p><ul><li>Diesel Propulsion Systems</li><li>Electric Locomotive Propulsion Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway propulsion systems market can be divided into two primary types: diesel propulsion systems and electric locomotive propulsion systems. Diesel propulsion systems rely on diesel engines to generate power, offering flexibility and the ability to operate on non-electrified tracks. In contrast, electric locomotive propulsion systems utilize electricity, either from overhead lines or a third rail, enabling higher speeds, reduced emissions, and lower operating costs. Each system caters to different operational needs and infrastructure availability in the rail industry.</p></p>
