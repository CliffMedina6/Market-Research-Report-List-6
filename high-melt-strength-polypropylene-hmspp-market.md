<p><strong>The High Melt Strength Polypropylene (HMSPP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymer</li><li>Copolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Melt Strength Polypropylene (HMSPP) is available in two main types: homopolymer and copolymer. Homopolymer HMSPP is a type of polypropylene that is made up of only propylene monomer units, while copolymer HMSPP is a blend of propylene monomer units and one or more other monomers. Homopolymer HMSPP offers higher stiffness and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring these properties. On the other hand, copolymer HMSPP provides improved impact resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for applications where these characteristics are crucial.</p></p>
