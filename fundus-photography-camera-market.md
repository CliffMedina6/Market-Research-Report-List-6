<p><p>Fundus photography cameras are a type of imaging device used to capture detailed images of the back of the eye, known as the fundus. There are different types of fundus photography cameras available in the market, such as OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) and Slit Lamps. OCT cameras use light waves to create cross-sectional images of the retina, while Slit Lamps are used for examining the anterior and posterior segments of the eye. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions.</p></p>
