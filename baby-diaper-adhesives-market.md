<p><strong>The Baby Diaper Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBC Based Adhesive</li><li>APAO Based Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Baby Diaper Adhesives market primarily consists of two types: SBC (Styrenic Block Copolymer) based adhesives and APAO (Amorphous Polyalphaolefin) based adhesives. SBC-based adhesives are known for their excellent elasticity and adhesion properties, making them ideal for securing diaper components. APAO-based adhesives, on the other hand, offer superior thermal stability and enhanced moisture resistance, providing a reliable bond under varying conditions. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring diaper performance, comfort, and functionality for consumers.</p></p>
