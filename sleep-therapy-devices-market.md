<p><strong>The Sleep Therapy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure)</li><li>BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure)</li><li>VPAP (Variable Positive Airway Pressure)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sleep therapy devices market includes CPAP, BiPAP, and VPAP devices. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) provides a constant flow of air to keep airways open during sleep. BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) delivers different air pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation. VPAP (Variable Positive Airway Pressure) adjusts air pressure based on the patient's breathing patterns. These devices are used to treat sleep apnea and other sleep-related breathing disorders by providing continuous or variable air pressure to keep airways open and improve breathing.</p></p>
