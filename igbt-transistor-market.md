<p><ul><li>Three-Terminal Monomer Encapsulation</li><li>IGBT and FWD Encapsulation Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IGBT transistor market is primarily categorized into two types: three-terminal monomer encapsulation and IGBT with freewheeling diode (FWD) encapsulation combination. Three-terminal monomer encapsulation involves individual IGBT devices packaged with three terminals, providing flexibility and ease of integration in various applications. On the other hand, the IGBT and FWD encapsulation combination integrates an IGBT with a freewheeling diode in a single package, enhancing efficiency and performance by reducing voltage stress and improving thermal management in power electronics.</p></p>
