<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Glass Market consists of various types including Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic Smart Glass can change its tint or opacity with the application of an electric current, providing control over the amount of light and heat entering a space. SPD Smart Glass utilizes suspended particle technology to adjust its opacity in response to an electric current. PDLC Smart Glass uses liquid crystal technology to switch between transparent and opaque states, offering privacy and energy efficiency benefits.</p></p>
