<p><p>The Battery Module Sensors market focuses on monitoring and managing battery performance in electric and hybrid vehicles. Key communication protocols include LIN (Local Interconnect Network) and CAN (Controller Area Network). LIN is a low-cost, simple network for connecting sensors and actuators, while CAN offers higher-speed data transfer suitable for complex vehicle systems. Both types enhance energy efficiency, safety, and reliability by enabling real-time data exchange between battery modules and vehicle control units, ensuring optimal battery management.</p></p>
