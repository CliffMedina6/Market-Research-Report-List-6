<p><strong>The Aircraft Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Leasing</li><li>Wet Leasing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft leasing market primarily consists of two types: dry leasing and wet leasing. Dry leasing involves the rental of an aircraft without crew, maintenance, or insurance, allowing airlines to operate the aircraft independently. This is often chosen for long-term needs. In contrast, wet leasing includes the aircraft, crew, maintenance, and insurance provided by the lessor, making it ideal for short-term requirements or during peak demand periods. Both options offer flexibility for airlines to manage their fleet efficiently.</p></p>
