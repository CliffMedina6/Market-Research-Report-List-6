<p><p>Helicobacter pylori testing without endoscopy includes different types of non-invasive tests such as stool/fecal antigen test, urea breath test, and H. pylori antibody testing. The stool/fecal antigen test detects H. pylori proteins in the stool, the urea breath test measures the presence of H. pylori based on the patient's breath samples, and the antibody testing detects antibodies produced by the body in response to H. pylori infection. These tests provide effective and convenient alternatives to traditional endoscopy for diagnosing H. pylori infection.</p></p>
