Ventilators provide mechanical ventilation for infants who cannot breathe adequately on their own. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure Devices deliver a constant flow of air to keep airways open. Apnea Monitors track a baby's breathing and alert caregivers to any pauses in breathing.</p></p>
