<p><p>Honeycomb ceramics are specialized materials used in automobile exhaust treatment to improve emission control and enhance engine performance. The market comprises two main types: straight-through carriers and wall-flow carriers. Straight-through carriers facilitate a linear flow of exhaust gases, allowing for less back pressure and improved performance. In contrast, wall-flow carriers trap pollutants within their porous walls, enabling efficient filtration of harmful emissions. Both types play a critical role in meeting regulatory standards and promoting environmental sustainability in the automotive industry.</p></p>
