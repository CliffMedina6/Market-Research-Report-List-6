<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Handheld Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID Handheld Reader</li><li>HF RFID Handheld Reader</li><li>UHF RFID Handheld Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID handheld reader market is categorized into three main types based on radio frequency: LF (Low Frequency), HF (High Frequency), and UHF (Ultra High Frequency). LF RFID handheld readers operate at frequencies around 125 kHz and are typically used for tracking livestock or access control. HF RFID readers, operating around 13.56 MHz, are commonly utilized in library management and ticketing systems. UHF RFID handheld readers, operating between 860-960 MHz, offer longer range and fast data transfer, making them ideal for inventory management and logistics.</p></p>
