<p><ul><li>Thermal DIN Rail Circuit Breakers</li><li>Magnetic DIN Rail Circuit Breakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DIN Circuit Protection market consists of two primary types: Thermal DIN Rail Circuit Breakers and Magnetic DIN Rail Circuit Breakers. Thermal breakers protect against overloads by using a bimetallic strip that bends with heat generated by excessive current, automatically tripping the circuit. In contrast, Magnetic breakers respond instantaneously to short circuits by utilizing an electromagnet that activates the tripping mechanism. Both types are widely used in industrial and commercial applications for reliable circuit protection and safety.</p></p>
