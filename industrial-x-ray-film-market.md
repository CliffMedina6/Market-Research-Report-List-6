<p><strong>The Industrial X-ray Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screen Type Films</li><li>Non-Screen Type Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial X-ray film market is primarily categorized into two types: screen type films and non-screen type films. Screen type films are designed to be used with intensifying screens, enhancing sensitivity and reducing exposure time, making them ideal for applications like weld inspections and structural evaluations. In contrast, non-screen type films do not rely on screens for exposure, providing higher image detail and contrast, which is essential for applications requiring precise evaluations, such as detecting internal flaws in materials.</p></p>
