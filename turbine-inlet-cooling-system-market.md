<p><p>Turbine Inlet Cooling Systems are used to enhance the performance and efficiency of gas turbines by cooling the air entering the system. The three main types of Turbine Inlet Cooling Systems are Inlet Fogging, Chiller System, and Evaporative Cooling. Inlet Fogging involves injecting water into the turbine inlet to cool the air, while Chiller Systems use refrigeration to lower the temperature. Evaporative Cooling systems utilize the evaporation of water to cool the incoming air. These systems help increase power output and efficiency in gas turbines.</p></p>
