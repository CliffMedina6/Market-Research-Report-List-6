<p><strong>The Deep Ultraviolet (DUV) Lithography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>KrF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Deep Ultraviolet (DUV) lithography systems market includes three primary technologies: ArF Immersion, ArF Dry, and KrF. ArF Immersion systems use an ultraviolet light wavelength of 193 nm and immerse the wafer in a liquid to enhance resolution, making them suitable for advanced node manufacturing. ArF Dry systems, also utilizing 193 nm light, operate in air and are often employed for less critical layers. KrF systems use 248 nm wavelength technology, typically used for older, less complex semiconductor processes. Each type serves different manufacturing needs and technology nodes.</p></p>
