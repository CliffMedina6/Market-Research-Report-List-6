<p><p>Superdisintegrants market can be categorized into different types such as SSG, XP, CCS, L-HPC, and others. SSG (Sodium Starch Glycolate) is widely used due to its superior disintegration properties. XP (Crospovidone) is known for its excellent swelling ability. CCS (Croscarmellose Sodium) provides good disintegration and dissolution enhancement. L-HPC (Low-substituted Hydroxypropyl Cellulose) is preferred for its fast disintegration and low hygroscopicity. Other superdisintegrants include materials like starch and cellulose derivatives that also contribute to rapid disintegration.</p></p>
