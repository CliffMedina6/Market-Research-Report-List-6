<p><p>The Polymer Neurovascular Stent market comprises primarily two types: carotid artery stents and intracranial stents. Carotid artery stents are designed to treat narrowing in the carotid arteries to prevent strokes, while intracranial stents are used for conditions within the brain's blood vessels, such as aneurysms or blockages. Both types utilize advanced polymer materials, enhancing biocompatibility and flexibility, which promotes better patient outcomes and reduces complications during neurovascular interventions, contributing to the overall growth of the market.</p></p>
