<p><p>Evacuation slide systems are categorized into two main types: single lane and double lane. Single lane systems consist of one lane for evacuees to slide down in the event of an emergency, while double lane systems have two parallel lanes, allowing for a larger number of people to evacuate at the same time. Single lane systems are typically used in smaller buildings or aircraft, while double lane systems are preferred in larger spaces where a larger number of people need to evacuate quickly.</p></p>
