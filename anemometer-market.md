<p><p>Anemometer market consists of various types, including Velocity Anemometer and Pressure Anemometer. Velocity Anemometer measures the speed at which air is moving, providing information on wind speed. Pressure Anemometer, on the other hand, measures the pressure exerted by the air, which can be used to determine air velocity. Both types of anemometers are essential tools for meteorological studies, environmental monitoring, and various industries where precise airflow measurements are required.</p></p>
