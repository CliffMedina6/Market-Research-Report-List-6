<p><p>The nuclear medicine/radiopharmaceutical market includes various radioisotopes used for diagnostics and treatment. Indium-111 (In-111) is utilized in tumor imaging. Xenon-133 (Xe-133) is primarily used for lung ventilation studies, while Thallium-201 (Th-201) is important for cardiac imaging. Gallium-67 (Ga-67) is employed in detecting infections and tumors, while "Other" refers to various emerging isotopes in clinical applications.</p></p>
