<p><p>Thermal spray coating equipment is divided into several types based on the technology used for the coating process. Plasma spray involves heating a gas to create a plasma that melts the coating material. HVOF spray uses high velocity oxygen-fuel to spray a coating material onto a surface. Arc spray technology relies on an electric arc to heat and melt the coating material. Other types of thermal spray coating equipment include flame spray and detonation spray, each offering unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
