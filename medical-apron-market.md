<p><p>The medical apron market is segmented into two main types: reusable and disposable aprons. Reusable aprons are designed for multiple uses, typically made from durable materials that can withstand repeated washing and decontamination. They are cost-effective for healthcare facilities aiming to reduce waste. In contrast, disposable aprons are single-use, made from lightweight, impermeable materials, providing convenience and hygiene. They are ideal for environments with high infection control standards, as they minimize cross-contamination risks. Both types serve essential roles in safeguarding healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
