Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Sensors measure gas levels by detecting infrared light absorption specific to gas molecules. Catalytic Gas Sensors identify LPG through reactions on a catalyst surface, generating heat detectable as a change in resistance. Other sensors may encompass various technologies, broadening detection capabilities, and enhancing safety in environments where LPG is used.</p></p>
