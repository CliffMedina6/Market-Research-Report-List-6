<p><p>The laboratory spray drying machine market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Spray Dryer**: Utilizes high pressure to atomize liquid into fine droplets, producing uniform powders suitable for heat-sensitive materials. </p><p>2. **Stream Spray Dryer**: Employs high-velocity air streams to disperse the liquid, allowing rapid drying and efficient powder recovery. </p><p>3. **Centrifuge Spray Dryer**: Uses centrifugal force to spin the liquid, generating fine particles with consistent size, optimizing drying for various applications. Each type serves distinct needs in research and product development.</p></p>
