<p><p>Carbon fiber filament is widely used in the manufacturing industry, with two main market types: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves melting the filament and injecting it into a mold to create a desired shape. This process is used for producing complex and intricate parts. On the other hand, extrusion molding involves forcing the filament through a die to create a continuous shape. This process is commonly used in making tubes, pipes, and other linear shapes.</p></p>
