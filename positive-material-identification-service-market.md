<p><strong>The Positive Material Identification Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Fluorescence (XRF)</li><li>Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES)</li><li>Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Positive Material Identification Service market is segmented into three main types: X-ray Fluorescence (XRF), Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES), and Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS). XRF utilizes X-ray beams to analyze the elemental composition of materials. OES uses sparks generated by electrical discharges to identify elements in a sample. LIBS involves focusing a laser on a material, creating a plasma that emits light for analysis. Each method offers unique capabilities for identifying materials in various industries.</p></p>
