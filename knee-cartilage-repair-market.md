<p><p>The knee cartilage repair market encompasses various treatment types for addressing cartilage damage. Osteochondral grafts transplantation replaces damaged areas with healthy cartilage and bone. Cell-based cartilage resurfacing employs engineered cells to restore surface integrity. Implants transplantation involves using synthetic materials to support damaged areas, while microfracture stimulates cartilage growth through tiny holes in the bone. Additional products complement these primary treatments.</p></p>
