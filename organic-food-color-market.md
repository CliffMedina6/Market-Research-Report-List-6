<p><strong>The Organic Food Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Soluble</li><li>Oil-Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic food color market is categorized primarily into water-soluble and oil-soluble types. Water-soluble colors dissolve easily in water-based products, making them suitable for beverages, dairy, and baked goods. They offer vibrant hues and are often derived from sources like fruits and vegetables. In contrast, oil-soluble colors are used in products with high oil content, such as confectioneries and fatty foods. These colors are typically extracted from plant materials and provide stability and vivid coloration in oil-based formulations.</p></p>
