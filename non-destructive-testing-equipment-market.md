<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment Market encompasses various methodologies used to evaluate the integrity of materials without causing damage. X-Ray testing involves imaging to detect internal flaws, while Ultrasonic testing uses high-frequency sound waves for precision assessment. Magnetic Particle testing detects surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials, and Penetrant Flaw Detection reveals surface-breaking flaws by applying dye. Eddy Current testing evaluates conductive materials by measuring electromagnetic fields. Other NDT methods include visual inspection and thermography, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
