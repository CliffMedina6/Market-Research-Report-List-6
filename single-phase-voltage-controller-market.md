<p><strong>The Single Phase Voltage Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Phase Half-wave Voltage Controller</li><li>Single-Phase Full-wave Voltage Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Phase Voltage Controller market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Phase Half-wave Voltage Controllers and Single-Phase Full-wave Voltage Controllers. </p><p>Single-Phase Half-wave Controllers work by allowing current to pass through only one half of the AC waveform, effectively reducing the average voltage and power delivered to the load. In contrast, Single-Phase Full-wave Controllers utilize both halves of the AC cycle, providing a more efficient voltage regulation and smoother output. Both types are used in various applications for managing power supply and energy efficiency.</p></p>
