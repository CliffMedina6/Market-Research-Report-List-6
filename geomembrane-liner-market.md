<p><p>The geomembrane liner market comprises several key types, each tailored for specific applications. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers high strength and chemical resistance, making it ideal for landfills and mining. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and puncture-resistant, suitable for various containment applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and versatile, popular in water retention and waste containment. FPP (Flexible Polypropylene) is noted for its flexibility and UV resistance, while other materials include EVA and geosynthetic clay liners, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
