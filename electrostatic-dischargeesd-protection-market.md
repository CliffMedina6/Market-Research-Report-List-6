<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Protection market includes two main types: Ceramic Base and Semiconductor Base. Ceramic base ESD protection devices offer high voltage clamping capability and fast response times, making them ideal for applications where transient voltage spikes are a concern. Semiconductor base ESD protection devices, on the other hand, provide low capacitance and leakage current, making them suitable for high-speed data lines and sensitive electronic components. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge.</p></p>
