Constant Current Drivers maintain a consistent output current, ensuring uniform brightness across LED modules, making them ideal for applications requiring precise light levels. In contrast, Constant Voltage Drivers provide a fixed output voltage, allowing flexibility in LED configurations and easier system integration. Both types cater to distinct applications, influencing performance, efficiency, and design intricacies in LED lighting solutions.</p></p>
