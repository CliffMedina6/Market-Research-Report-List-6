<p><p>Color contact lenses are available in three main types based on the tint they have: visibility tint, enhancement tint, and opaque tint. Visibility tint lenses have a slight blue or green tint to help with easier handling and locating the lenses in the solution. Enhancement tint lenses are slightly translucent and are used to enhance the natural color of the eyes. Opaque tint lenses completely change the color of the eyes and are popular for those looking for a dramatic change in eye color.</p></p>
