<p><strong>The Hot Melt Structural Waterproofing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reinforced Bituminous Membrane</li><li>Monolithic Membrane</li><li>Mastic Asphalt Membrane</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot Melt Structural Waterproofing Systems are primarily categorized into four types: Reinforced Bituminous Membrane, Monolithic Membrane, Mastic Asphalt Membrane, and Others. Reinforced Bituminous Membrane systems consist of bitumen sheets reinforced with polyester or glass-fiber. Monolithic Membranes are seamless systems that provide excellent waterproofing protection. Mastic Asphalt Membranes are made of hot-applied asphalt and provide high flexibility and durability. Other types of systems include liquid applied membranes, spray-applied membranes, and cementitious waterproofing. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
