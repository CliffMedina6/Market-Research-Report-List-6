<p><p>DC Optimizers are categorized into two market types: Internal and External Installation. Internal Installation refers to DC optimizers integrated within the solar system's design, often built into the solar panels themselves, enhancing efficiency and streamlining installation. In contrast, External Installation involves separate devices installed alongside existing solar systems, optimizing performance without requiring panel replacements. Both types aim to maximize energy output, improve shading performance, and enhance system monitoring, catering to diverse consumer needs and installation scenarios in the solar energy market.</p></p>
