<p><p>Molded Interconnect Devices (MID) Market consists of various types, including Laser Direct Structuring (LDS), Two-Shot Molding, and others. LDS technology involves using a laser to write circuit patterns on the molded part, enabling complex three-dimensional circuit designs. Two-Shot Molding utilizes a two-step process to create integrated components with different materials. The "Others" market category includes additional MID manufacturing methods, such as 3D printing and film insert molding, offering diverse options for creating electronic components with integrated circuitry.</p></p>
