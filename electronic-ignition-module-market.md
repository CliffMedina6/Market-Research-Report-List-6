<p><strong>The Electronic Ignition Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single coil</li><li>Multi-coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Ignition Module market is broadly categorized into two types: single coil and multi-coil systems. Single coil systems utilize a single ignition coil to fire all cylinders, making them simpler and more cost-effective, often favored in smaller engines. Multi-coil systems, on the other hand, employ individual coils for each cylinder, enhancing performance, fuel efficiency, and emission control by providing more precise ignition timing. This differentiation drives innovations and preferences in various automotive applications based on performance needs and engine complexity.</p></p>
