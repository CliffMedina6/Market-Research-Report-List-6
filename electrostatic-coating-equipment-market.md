<p><ul><li>Liquid Electrostatic Coating Equipment</li><li>Powder Electrostatic Coating Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic coating equipment market includes two main types: liquid electrostatic coating equipment and powder electrostatic coating equipment. Liquid electrostatic coating equipment applies a charged liquid coating to a conductive surface, which is attracted to the surface and forms a smooth, even coating. Powder electrostatic coating equipment applies a charged powder coating to a grounded surface, which adheres through electrostatic attraction and is then cured to form a durable finish. Both types offer efficient and versatile coating solutions for various industries.</p></p>
