<p><strong>The Semiconductor Mask Blanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Mask</li><li>Soda Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor mask blanks market primarily consists of two types: quartz masks and soda-lime masks. Quartz masks are made from high-purity silicon dioxide, offering excellent optical properties and durability, making them suitable for advanced photolithography processes. In contrast, soda-lime masks, composed of soda-lime glass, are more cost-effective but have lower thermal stability and optical clarity. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor production, with quartz masks typically used for high-performance applications and soda-lime masks for less demanding processes.</p></p>
