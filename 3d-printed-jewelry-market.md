<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 3D Printed Jewelry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SLA Technology</li><li>SLS Technology</li><li>DLP Technology</li><li>FDM Technology</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printed jewelry market comprises several technologies, each with unique characteristics. SLA (Stereolithography) uses UV light to cure resin into intricate designs, offering high detail and smooth finishes. SLS (Selective Laser Sintering) employs a laser to fuse powdered materials, suitable for producing durable and complex shapes. DLP (Digital Light Processing) also uses light to solidify resin but is typically faster than SLA. FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) extrudes thermoplastic filaments, offering affordability but lower detail. Other technologies include binder jetting and polymer jetting, each catering to specific design and material needs.</p></p>
