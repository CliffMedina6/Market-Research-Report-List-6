<p><strong>The Cooling Tower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporative Cooling Tower</li><li>Dry Cooling Tower</li><li>Hybrid Cooling Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooling towers are used in industrial processes to remove excess heat through evaporation of water. The Evaporative Cooling Tower uses water and air to cool the process fluid. Dry Cooling Towers use air to cool the process fluid without direct contact with water, making them ideal for water-sensitive applications. Hybrid Cooling Towers combine both evaporative and dry cooling methods for optimal efficiency. Each type of cooling tower offers unique benefits and is chosen based on factors such as water availability, environmental regulations, and energy efficiency requirements.</p></p>
