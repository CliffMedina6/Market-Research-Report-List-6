<p><p>The 3D sensor market comprises several key technologies: </p><p>1. **Stereo Vision** uses two cameras to capture images from different angles, mimicking human depth perception for object recognition and tracking.</p><p>2. **Structured Light** projects known patterns onto a scene, analyzing distortions to calculate depth and shape.</p><p>3. **Time of Flight (ToF)** sensors emit light pulses and measure the time taken for reflections to return, allowing precise distance measurements. </p><p>4. **Ultrasound** employs sound waves to measure distances, commonly used in medical imaging and industrial applications. Each type has unique applications and benefits, catering to various industries.</p></p>
