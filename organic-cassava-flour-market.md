<p><p>Organic Cassava Flour market is divided into two main types - Sweet Cassava Flour and Bitter Cassava Flour. On the other hand, Bitter Cassava Flour is made from the bitter variety of cassava plant and is primarily used in savory dishes for its slightly bitter taste. Both types of cassava flour are gaining popularity in the health food market for their gluten-free and nutrient-rich properties.</p></p>
