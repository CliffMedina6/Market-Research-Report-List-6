<p><p>The cellular-connected drone market is categorized into two primary types: BVLOS (Beyond Visual Line of Sight) and VLOS (Visual Line of Sight). BVLOS refers to operations where drones fly beyond the operator's visual range, using cellular networks for real-time data and control, enabling extended missions like deliveries or surveillance. In contrast, VLOS involves flying drones within the operator's sight, primarily for recreational or short-range commercial applications, leveraging direct control and enhanced safety in operations.</p></p>
