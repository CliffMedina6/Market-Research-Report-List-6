<p><p>The High-voltage MOSFET Market includes three main types: Junction Tube MOSFET, Insulated Gate MOSFET, and Others. Junction Tube MOSFETs have a structure where the gate is integrated with the substrate material. Insulated Gate MOSFETs have a layer of insulating material between the gate and the channel, allowing for higher voltage operation. The Others category includes various MOSFET designs that do not fall into the specific categories of Junction Tube or Insulated Gate, providing alternative options for high-voltage applications.</p></p>
