<p><ul><li>Non Interlock</li><li>Single Interlock</li><li>Double Interlock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Preaction Sprinkler Systems are commonly categorized into three market types based on their activation method: Non Interlock, Single Interlock, and Double Interlock. Non Interlock systems operate independently without any interconnection between the sprinkler heads. Single Interlock systems require a fire detection system to activate, while Double Interlock systems need both a fire detection system and a sprinkler head activation to release water. Each market type offers different levels of precision and control in fire protection based on the specific needs of the building or environment.</p></p>
