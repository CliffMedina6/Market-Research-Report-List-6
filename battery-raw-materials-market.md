<p><p>The Battery Raw Materials Market can be categorized into three main types: Anode market, Cathode market, and Separator market. </p><p>The Anode market involves materials like graphite, silicon, and lithium that store energy in lithium-ion batteries. The Cathode market includes materials such as lithium, cobalt, and nickel that facilitate the flow of electrons in batteries. </p><p>The Separator market involves materials that physically separate the anode and cathode to prevent short circuits and enable the flow of ions. These markets play a crucial role in the production and performance of batteries.</p></p>
