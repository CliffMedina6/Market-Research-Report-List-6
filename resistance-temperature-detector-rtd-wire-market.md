<p><p>The Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD) wire market can be categorized into two main types: thin-film RTDs and wire-wound RTDs. Thin-film RTDs utilize a thin layer of conductive material deposited on a ceramic substrate, offering fast response times and good accuracy for various applications. In contrast, wire-wound RTDs consist of a coiled wire element, providing excellent stability and precision, especially in high-temperature environments. Both types are widely used in industrial processes, HVAC systems, and laboratory settings for temperature measurement.</p></p>
