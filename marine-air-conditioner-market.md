<p><p>The Marine Air Conditioner market primarily features two types: Integral and Split Type Air Conditioners. In contrast, Split Type Air Conditioners consist of two separate units—an indoor and an outdoor unit—allowing for more efficient cooling and flexibility in installation across larger vessels. Both types cater to the specific cooling needs of maritime environments, enhancing comfort on board.</p></p>
