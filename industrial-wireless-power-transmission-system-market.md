Electro Magnetic Induction Technology uses electromagnetic fields to transfer power between coils, while Magnetic Field Coupling Technology uses magnetic fields to transfer power wirelessly. These technologies eliminate the need for physical connection between devices, allowing for efficient and convenient power transmission in industrial settings. They are increasingly being adopted for various applications such as charging electric vehicles, powering sensors, and enabling automation in factories.</p></p>
