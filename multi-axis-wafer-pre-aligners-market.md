<p><p>Multi Axis Wafer Pre Aligners are essential tools used in semiconductor manufacturing to align wafers before processing. There are two main types of these aligners: Vacuum Aligners and Atmosphere Aligners. Vacuum Aligners provide a high level of precision and stability by removing air between the wafer and chuck, ensuring accurate alignment. On the other hand, Atmosphere Aligners operate in a controlled environment with gas or air, offering a more flexible approach to wafer alignment while maintaining precision. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient semiconductor production processes.</p></p>
