<p><p>Fixed-installation projectors are designed for permanent installation in a specific location such as a conference room or auditorium. They offer high brightness and resolution for large-scale projections. On the other hand, Pico projectors are small, portable projectors that are ideal for on-the-go presentations or entertainment. They are lightweight and compact, making them easy to transport. Both types of projectors cater to different market needs, with fixed-installation projectors targeting larger venues while Pico projectors are more suited for personal or small-group use.</p></p>
