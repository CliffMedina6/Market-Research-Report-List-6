<p><p>The microsuspension polyvinyl chloride (PVC) market is categorized into two types: homogeneous and heterogeneous. In contrast, heterogeneous type involves a distinct separation within the mixture, resulting in varied properties and potential for stronger mechanical attributes. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing the versatility of PVC applications.</p></p>
