<p><p>Advanced Prosthetics and Exoskeleton Market includes three main types: Lower, Upper, and Full Body prosthetics and exoskeletons. Lower body prosthetics are designed to replace or enhance functionality in the legs, knees, and feet for individuals with lower limb amputations. Upper body prosthetics focus on replacing or enhancing functionality in the arms, hands, and shoulders for individuals with upper limb amputations. Full body prosthetics and exoskeletons offer comprehensive support for individuals with both upper and lower limb amputations, providing a complete solution for mobility and independence.</p></p>
