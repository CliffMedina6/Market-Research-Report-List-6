<p><p>The commercial and household water purification systems market encompasses various filtration technologies. KDF filter media targets heavy metals and bacteria. Other media filtration includes options like ceramic and sand filters. Reverse osmosis membranes provide thorough purification by removing dissolved solids. UV disinfection utilizes ultraviolet light to eliminate pathogens. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies and hybrid systems for diverse purification needs.</p></p>
