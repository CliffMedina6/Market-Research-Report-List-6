<p><p>The sterile medical plastic packaging market comprises two primary types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes materials like pouches and bags, which are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer excellent barrier properties, making them ideal for items like sterilized instruments and medical devices. Rigid packaging involves materials such as bottles, containers, and trays that provide structural strength and protection for products during transportation and storage. Both types are essential in maintaining sterility and ensuring the safety of medical supplies.</p></p>
