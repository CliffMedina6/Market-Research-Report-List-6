<p><strong>The Contact Smart Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active card</li><li>Passive card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contact smart cards market is categorized into active and passive cards. Active cards have an internal power source, enabling them to process information independently and support advanced functionalities like biometrics or wireless communication. In contrast, passive cards derive power from the card reader, offering simpler operations primarily for identification or access control. While active cards tend to be more versatile and feature-rich, passive cards are often more cost-effective, making both types suitable for various applications across industries.</p></p>
