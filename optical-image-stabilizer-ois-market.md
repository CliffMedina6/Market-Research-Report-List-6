<p><strong>The Optical Image Stabilizer (OIS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lens Shift Method</li><li>Module Tilt Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Image Stabilization (OIS) technology in camera systems can be categorized into two types: Lens Shift Method and Module Tilt Method. </p><p>1. Lens Shift Method: In this method, the lens elements inside the camera move to counteract any shake or vibration, ensuring a stable image is captured.</p><p>2. Module Tilt Method: This method involves tilting the entire camera module to compensate for any movement, providing a steady image output. Both methods are designed to improve the quality of images and reduce blurriness caused by camera shake.</p></p>
