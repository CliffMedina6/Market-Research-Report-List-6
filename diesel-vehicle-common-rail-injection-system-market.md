<p><strong>The Diesel Vehicle Common Rail Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low pressure pump system</li><li>High pressure pump system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diesel Vehicle Common Rail Injection System market is primarily divided into two types: low pressure pump systems and high pressure pump systems. Low pressure pump systems maintain fuel delivery at lower pressures, ensuring a steady supply to the common rail. In contrast, high pressure pump systems generate the intense pressures necessary for creating fine fuel atomization, enhancing combustion efficiency and performance. Both systems are integral to optimizing engine performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions control in diesel vehicles.</p></p>
