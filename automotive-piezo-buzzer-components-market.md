<p><p>The automotive piezo buzzer components market is segmented into active and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers have built-in oscillators, requiring only a DC voltage to produce sound, making them compact and efficient for alert signals in vehicles. Conversely, passive piezo buzzers require an external audio signal to generate sound, offering flexibility in tone generation through varying frequencies. Both types are essential for alerts, notifications, and indication signals in modern automotive applications, enhancing safety and user experience.</p></p>
