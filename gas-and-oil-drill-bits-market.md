<p><ul><li>Fixed Cutter</li><li>Roller Cone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas and Oil Drill Bits market includes two main types of bits: Fixed Cutter and Roller Cone bits. Fixed Cutter bits have stationary teeth that shear rock formations to create boreholes, making them ideal for hard formations. Roller Cone bits have rotating cones with teeth that crush rock formations, making them versatile for different formations. Both types have distinct advantages and disadvantages, with Fixed Cutter bits offering higher durability and Roller Cone bits providing better performance in softer formations.</p></p>
