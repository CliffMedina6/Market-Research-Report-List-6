<p><p>Optically Pumped Magnetometers (OPM) are devices that measure magnetic fields using light to manipulate electron spins. There are two main types of OPMs: Potassium Magnetometers and Cesium Magnetometers. </p><p>Potassium Magnetometers use a potassium vapor cell to detect magnetic fields, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, Cesium Magnetometers use a cesium vapor cell and are known for their stability and resistance to external disturbances.</p><p>Both types of OPMs are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, defense, and medical research.</p></p>
