<p><p>The prescription glasses market primarily features two types: single vision and progressive lenses. Single vision prescription glasses have a uniform focal length, designed to correct either nearsightedness or farsightedness, making them suitable for specific visual needs. In contrast, progressive prescription glasses offer a seamless transition between multiple viewing distances, accommodating nearsightedness, farsightedness, and presbyopia without visible lines. This versatility makes progressive lenses ideal for individuals requiring multifocal vision correction, enhancing both functionality and aesthetic appeal in eyewear.</p></p>
