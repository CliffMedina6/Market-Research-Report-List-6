<p><p>The automotive exhaust components market includes essential parts such as the manifold, muffler, clamps, stack pipe, manifold pipe, and connectors. The manifold collects exhaust gases from the engine. The muffler reduces noise and controls emissions. Clamps secure various parts together, ensuring a leak-proof system. Stack pipes direct exhaust upward, while manifold pipes connect the exhaust system to the engine. Connectors facilitate the seamless joining of different exhaust sections, maintaining efficiency and performance in vehicle emissions control systems.</p></p>
