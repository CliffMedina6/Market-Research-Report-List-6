<p><p>Process oils are categorized into different market types based on their chemical composition and properties. </p><p>Naphthenic process oils are known for their excellent solvency and low volatility, making them suitable for rubber and tire manufacturing. Paraffinic oils have high viscosity index and thermal stability, making them ideal for lubricant and metalworking applications. Aromatic oils are valued for their high solvency and high viscosity, commonly used in ink, adhesives, and sealants. Other market types include different specialty oils tailored for specific applications such as plasticizers, defoamers, and antifoaming agents.</p></p>
