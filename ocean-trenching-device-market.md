<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Jet Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ocean trenching devices can be categorized into mechanical type and jet type markets. Mechanical type devices utilize rotating cutters or trenchers to dig into the ocean floor, while jet type devices use powerful water jets to create trenches. Both types of devices are used in various marine industries for activities such as laying cables, pipelines, and conducting scientific research. The mechanical type market is more commonly used for heavier-duty trenching tasks, while the jet type market is preferred for lighter, more precise trenching operations.</p></p>
