<p><p>The rubber ingredients market comprises various types essential for rubber production. Vulcanizing agents, like sulfur, improve elasticity and durability through cross-linking. Protective agents, such as antioxidants, prevent degradation from heat and oxidants. Reinforcing agents, primarily carbon black and silica, enhance strength and performance. Bonding agents improve adhesion between rubber and other materials, ensuring better product integrity. Other ingredients may include fillers, processing oils, and plasticizers, all contributing to the overall quality and functionality of rubber products.</p></p>
