<p><p>The Solar Radiation Smart Sensors Market is primarily categorized into two types: Silicon Cell Type and Thermopile Type. Silicon Cell Type sensors utilize silicon photovoltaic cells to convert solar energy into electrical signals, providing accurate measurements of solar irradiance. Conversely, Thermopile Type sensors measure temperature differences caused by solar radiation, converting these differences into voltage for quantifying solar energy. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing solar energy applications, enhancing efficiency in various renewable energy systems.</p></p>
