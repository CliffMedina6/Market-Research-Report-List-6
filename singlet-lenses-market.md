<p><p>Singlet lenses are categorized into different types based on their shapes and curvature. Plano-convex lenses have one flat surface and one convex surface, while Plano-concave lenses have one flat surface and one concave surface. Biconvex lenses have two convex surfaces, and Double Concave lenses have two concave surfaces. Other types of singlet lenses include meniscus lenses, cylindrical lenses, and aspheric lenses. Each type serves a specific purpose in applications such as imaging, focusing, and light manipulation.</p></p>
