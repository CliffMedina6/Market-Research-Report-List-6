<p><p>Diaphragm type metering pumps are essential in precision fluid transfer applications. They are categorized into two main types: mechanical diaphragm and hydraulic diaphragm pumps. Mechanical diaphragm pumps utilize a mechanical link to drive the diaphragm, offering simple operation and maintenance. In contrast, hydraulic diaphragm pumps employ hydraulic fluid to actuate the diaphragm, enabling higher pressures and flow rates. Both types ensure accurate flow control, minimizing leakage and contamination, making them ideal for chemical processing, water treatment, and other industries requiring reliable metering solutions.</p></p>
