<p><p>Gap Insurance is available in various market types to cater to different needs of consumers. Finance GAP Insurance covers the outstanding loan amount in case of total loss of the vehicle. Return-to-invoice GAP Insurance reimburses the difference between the insurance payout and the original invoice price. Vehicle Replacement GAP Insurance provides the funds for purchasing a new vehicle. Return-to-value GAP Insurance covers the difference between the insurance payout and the current market value. Other types of Gap Insurance offer additional benefits or customized coverage options.</p></p>
