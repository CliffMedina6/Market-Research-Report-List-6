<p><p>The exterior structural glazing market includes various types of glass prominently used in building applications. Insulating glass consists of multiple glass panes separated by a spacer, enhancing energy efficiency. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, making it ideal for high-stress environments. Low-e (low emissivity) glass features a special coating that reflects infrared energy, improving thermal performance while allowing natural light. Each type serves specific architectural needs while contributing to energy efficiency and structural integrity.</p></p>
