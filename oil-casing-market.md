<p><p>Oil casing is a steel pipe that is used to line the walls of a drilled oil well to prevent the collapse of the wellbore and to ensure that no oil or gas leaks out into the surrounding ground. It also provides structural support to the well while drilling, cementing and completing the well. Oil casing is an essential component in the oil and gas industry, as it ensures the safe and efficient extraction of oil and gas from the earth.</p><p>The Oil Casing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.6% during the forecast period.
