<p><p>Jewelry 3D Printing market types include Stereolithography (SLA), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), Digital Light Processing (DLP), Fused Depositing Modelling (FDM), and others. SLA uses liquid resin and UV light to create precise jewelry designs. SLS uses powdered materials and a laser to fuse layers together. DLP works similarly to SLA but uses a digital light projector. FDM extrudes thermoplastic materials to build jewelry pieces layer by layer. Other types may include Multi Jet Fusion (MJF) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM) for producing intricate and high-quality jewelry designs.</p></p>
