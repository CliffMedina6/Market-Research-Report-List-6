<p><strong>The Intravitreal Injectable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anti-VEGF</li><li>Steroids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravitreal injectable market primarily comprises two key types: Anti-VEGF and steroids. Anti-VEGF agents inhibit vascular endothelial growth factor, reducing abnormal blood vessel growth associated with conditions like age-related macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy. Steroids, on the other hand, address inflammation and swelling within the eye, providing relief in cases such as uveitis or retinal edema. Both types of injections are administered directly into the vitreous humor to achieve localized therapeutic effects, aiming to preserve vision and enhance ocular health.</p></p>
