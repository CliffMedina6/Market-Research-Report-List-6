Capacitive proximity sensors can sense both conductive and non-conductive materials, making them useful for level detection of liquids and granules. Magnetic proximity sensors detect magnetic fields and are often used in door and safety applications. Together, these technologies enhance automation and quality control in food processing and packaging environments.</p></p>
