<p><strong>The Viscosupplementation Injection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Injection</li><li>Three Injection</li><li>Five Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Viscosupplementation injections are categorized based on the number of injections required for treatment. Single injection treatments involve a single dose of hyaluronic acid administered to the joint. Three injection treatments consist of three separate shots given over the course of several weeks. Five injection treatments involve a more extended regimen, with five doses administered at regular intervals. The choice of injection type depends on the severity of joint pain and the patient's individual needs.</p></p>
