The Ku Band market operates at higher frequencies between 12 to 18 GHz, commonly used for direct broadcast satellite services. The Ka Band operates at even higher frequencies between 26.5 to 40 GHz, offering higher data rates for broadband services. The K Band market operates at frequencies around 18 to 27 GHz, used for fixed satellite services and military applications.</p></p>
