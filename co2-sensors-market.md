<p><strong>The CO2 Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical CO2 Sensors</li><li>NDIR CO2 Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 sensors market is primarily segmented into chemical CO2 sensors and NDIR CO2 sensors. Chemical CO2 sensors utilize chemical reactions to detect carbon dioxide concentrations, often featuring electrochemical mechanisms for measurement. In contrast, NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 sensors operate based on the absorption of infrared light by CO2 molecules, providing accurate and reliable measurements. Both types cater to various applications, including indoor air quality monitoring, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring, reflecting the growing demand for effective CO2 detection solutions.</p></p>
