<p><p>The oxygen nasal cannula market is categorized into two main types: low-flow oxygen nasal cannulas and high-flow oxygen nasal cannulas. Low-flow cannulas deliver oxygen at variable rates, suitable for patients needing supplemental oxygen in home care settings or during mild respiratory distress. In contrast, high-flow cannulas provide a consistent oxygen supply at higher flow rates, ideal for critically ill patients or those with severe respiratory conditions, ensuring adequate oxygenation and enhanced patient comfort during treatment.</p></p>
