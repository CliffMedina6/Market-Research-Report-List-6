<p><p>Wireless communication technologies in healthcare market types include Bluetooth, Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), Ultra Wide Band (UWB), WLAN and WWAN, Wi-Fi, and Li-Fi. UWB offers high-speed data transfer over short distances, while WLAN and WWAN provide wireless network connectivity. Wi-Fi is commonly used for internet access, and Li-Fi uses light to transmit data. These technologies play a crucial role in improving communication and efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
