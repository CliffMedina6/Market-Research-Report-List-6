<p><strong>The Aluminum Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum castings market primarily includes die casting and permanent mold casting. Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold under high pressure, suitable for high-volume production of complex shapes with excellent surface finish. Conversely, permanent mold casting utilizes reusable metal molds, allowing for better control over cooling rates and producing strong, precise parts, typically for medium production runs. Both methods offer unique advantages, catering to diverse industrial applications like automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods.</p></p>
