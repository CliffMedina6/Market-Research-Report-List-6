<p><strong>The Cellulase Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EG</li><li>CBH</li><li>BG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellulase market is segmented into three main types: endoglucanases (EG), cellobiohydrolases (CBH), and beta-glucosidases (BG). EG enzymes cleave internal bonds in cellulose, enhancing fiber accessibility. CBH enzymes act on the ends of cellulose chains, releasing cellobiose units. BG enzymes hydrolyze cellobiose into glucose, facilitating further degradation. Together, these enzymes play a crucial role in the breakdown of cellulose in various applications, including biofuels, animal feed, and textile industries, driving demand for effective cellulase solutions.</p></p>
