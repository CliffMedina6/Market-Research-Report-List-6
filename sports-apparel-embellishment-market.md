<p><p>The sports apparel embellishment market consists of several key techniques. Screen printing involves applying ink through stencils for vibrant designs, ideal for bulk production. Direct-to-Garment (DTG) printing allows intricate, full-color designs directly onto fabric, perfect for small orders. Heat transfer printing uses heat to apply designs from a carrier, suitable for complex visuals. Lastly, embroidery stitches designs onto fabric, offering a durable and textured finish popular for logos and personalization. Each technique serves diverse needs in customization.</p></p>
