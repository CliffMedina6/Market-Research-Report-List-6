<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Surveillance Cameras</li><li>Decentralized IP Surveillance Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IP surveillance camera market is divided into centralized and decentralized types. Centralized IP surveillance cameras rely on a central server for video storage and management, offering streamlined monitoring and easier integration with existing systems. In contrast, decentralized IP surveillance cameras operate independently, processing and storing video data on the camera itself or on edge devices. This allows for greater scalability and reduced reliance on a central infrastructure, making it suitable for locations with limited bandwidth or remote monitoring needs.</p></p>
