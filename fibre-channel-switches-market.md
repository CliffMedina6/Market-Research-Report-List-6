<p><p>Fibre Channel switches are categorized into two main market types: Modular Director Switches, also known as Backbone Switches, and Semi-modular Switches, also known as Edge Switches. Modular Director Switches are high-end switches commonly used in enterprise data centers to connect multiple servers and storage systems in a scalable and resilient manner. Semi-modular Switches are smaller switches typically used at the edge of the network to connect individual devices or small groups of devices to the larger network infrastructure.</p></p>
