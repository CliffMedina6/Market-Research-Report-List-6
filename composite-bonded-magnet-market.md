<p><p>Composite bonded magnets can be produced through two main types of manufacturing processes - injection molding and pressing molding. The injection molding market involves injecting a molten mixture of magnetic powders and a polymer binder into a mold at high pressure to create the desired shape. On the other hand, the pressing molding market involves compressing a mixture of magnetic powders and a binder into a mold using pressure to form the final magnet shape. Both processes are commonly used in the production of composite bonded magnets.</p></p>
