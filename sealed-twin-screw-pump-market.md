<p><p>The Sealed Twin-Screw Pump market is primarily segmented into Single Volute and Double Volute types. Single Volute Twin Screw Pumps feature a singular spiral chamber, allowing for consistent flow and efficient handling of various fluids. In contrast, Double Volute Twin Screw Pumps utilize two spiral chambers, which minimize radial forces and improve stability, making them ideal for high-viscosity fluids. Both types are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and food processing due to their reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
