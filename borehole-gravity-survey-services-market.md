<p><p>Borehole Gravity Survey Services Market includes two main types of gravity gradiometers- Absolute Gravity Gradiometer and Relative Gravity Gradiometer. Absolute Gravity Gradiometers measure the actual gravitational acceleration at a specific point, providing highly accurate data but requiring a stable environment. On the other hand, Relative Gravity Gradiometers measure the differences in gravitational acceleration between two points, offering cost-effective and convenient solutions for conducting gravity surveys. Both types of gradiometers are utilized in various industries such as oil and gas exploration, mineral exploration, and geotechnical applications.</p></p>
