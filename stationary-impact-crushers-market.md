<p><strong>The Stationary Impact Crushers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Shaft Impact</li><li>Vertical Shaft Impact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary impact crushers market is primarily segmented into two types: Horizontal Shaft Impact (HSI) crushers and Vertical Shaft Impact (VSI) crushers. HSI crushers utilize horizontal motion to crush materials, making them ideal for secondary and tertiary crushing with high efficiency and low wear rates. In contrast, VSI crushers employ a vertical axis for material reduction, excelling in producing high-quality sand and shaping aggregates. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, mining, and recycling.</p></p>
