<p><p>Vertical sulphur pumps are available in two main types - multi-stage pump and single-stage pump. </p><p>Multi-stage pumps are designed to provide higher pressure and are suitable for applications where sulphur needs to be pumped over long distances or to higher elevations. </p><p>On the other hand, single-stage pumps are simpler in design and are best suited for applications where lower pressure is sufficient and where the sulphur does not need to be pumped over a long distance. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in various industrial processes involving the handling of sulphur.</p></p>
