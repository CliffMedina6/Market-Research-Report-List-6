<p><strong>The Fingerprint Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint</li><li>Optical Fingerprint</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fingerprint chips market consists of three main types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint sensors use electrical signals to capture ridge patterns, offering high accuracy and durability. Optical fingerprint sensors capture images of fingerprints using light, providing a cost-effective solution but can be less secure. Ultrasonic fingerprint modules utilize ultrasonic waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, delivering enhanced security and performance, particularly in challenging conditions, making them suitable for advanced biometric applications.</p></p>
