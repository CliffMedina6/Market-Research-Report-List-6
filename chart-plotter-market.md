<p><strong>The Chart Plotter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPS Based Chart Plotter</li><li>GNSS Based Chart Plotter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chart plotter market comprises two primary types: GPS-based and GNSS-based chart plotters. GPS-based chart plotters utilize the Global Positioning System to provide precise navigation information, ideal for recreational boating and fishing. In contrast, GNSS-based chart plotters leverage multiple satellite systems, including GLONASS and Galileo, to enhance accuracy and reliability, especially in challenging environments. Both types serve critical roles in maritime navigation, with GNSS-based options catering to professional and commercial applications requiring higher precision.</p></p>
