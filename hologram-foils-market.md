Holographic Narrow Cold Foil is used for narrow web printing applications, providing a metallic and holographic finish. Holographic Composite Foil combines holographic and non-holographic elements for a unique visual effect. Each type caters to specific market needs and applications within the hologram foils industry.</p></p>
