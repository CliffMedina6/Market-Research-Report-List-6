<p><strong>The Chemical Indicator Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Sterilization</li><li>Ethylene Oxide (EO) Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical indicator tape is utilized in various sterilization processes, primarily steam sterilization and ethylene oxide (EO) sterilization. In steam sterilization, the tape changes color when exposed to high temperatures and moisture, confirming effective sterilization within autoclaves. Conversely, in EO sterilization, the tape indicates exposure to ethylene oxide gas, crucial for heat-sensitive medical instruments. Both types are vital for ensuring that sterilization protocols are properly followed, enhancing safety and reliability in healthcare settings.</p></p>
