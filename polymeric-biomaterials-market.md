<p><p>Polymeric biomaterials are materials that are used in the field of medicine and healthcare to construct medical devices and implants that come into direct contact with biological systems. Technological advancements in the field of biomaterials are also expected to contribute to market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the polymeric biomaterials market include the development of bioresorbable polymers, which are designed to be absorbed by the body over time, reducing the need for a second surgery to remove the implant. Additionally, there is a growing focus on the use of biodegradable polymers that break down naturally in the body, reducing the risk of infection and inflammation.</p><p>Overall, the polymeric biomaterials market is poised for significant growth in the coming years, driven by advancements in material science and increasing demand for biocompatible materials in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
