<p><p>The geosynthetics market comprises various types, including geotextiles, geomembranes, geogrids, and others. Geotextiles are permeable fabrics used in soil stabilization and erosion control. Geomembranes are impermeable liners for containment applications, crucial in landfills and water reservoirs. Geogrids provide structural reinforcement for soil and asphalt applications, improving load distribution. Other geosynthetic products include geomats, geocomposites, and geofoams, each serving specialized functions in civil engineering, environmental protection, and construction, enhancing durability and sustainability in projects.</p></p>
