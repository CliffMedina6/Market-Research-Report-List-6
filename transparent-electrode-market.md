<p><p>The transparent electrode market primarily consists of Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) and Carbon Nanotube (CNT) transparent electrodes. ITO electrodes are widely used in displays and solar cells due to their high conductivity and optical transparency but face challenges like material scarcity and brittleness. In contrast, CNT electrodes offer flexibility, low-cost production, and superior mechanical properties, making them suitable for emerging applications, including wearable electronics and flexible displays. Both types are crucial in advancing next-generation electronic devices.</p></p>
