<p><ul><li>Pre-Exposure Vaccine</li><li>Post-Exposure Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Human rabies vaccine is used for both pre-exposure and post-exposure purposes. Pre-exposure vaccination is recommended for individuals at high risk of exposure to rabies, such as veterinarians or laboratory workers. It helps to provide immunity before exposure to the virus. Post-exposure vaccination is administered to individuals who have been bitten by an animal suspected of carrying rabies. It helps to prevent the virus from infecting the individual and developing into the disease. Both applications are crucial in preventing the spread of rabies among humans.</p></p>
