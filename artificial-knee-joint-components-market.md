<p><p>The artificial knee joint components market consists of various types of components including the femoral end implant, tibia articular surface liner, tibia base, patella implant, and other components. The femoral end implant is a metal or ceramic component that replaces the end of the thigh bone, while the tibia articular surface liner is a plastic or metal component that covers the top of the shin bone. The tibia base provides a stable base for the knee joint, and the patella implant replaces the kneecap. Other components in the market may include screws, spacers, and ligament stabilizers.</p></p>
