<p><p>Defibrillator rental market includes three main types: Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs), Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), and Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs). AEDs are portable devices used for sudden cardiac arrest emergencies. ICDs are implanted in the chest to monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks if needed. WCDs are worn outside the body and monitor heart rhythms continuously, providing early intervention for arrhythmias. Each type serves a specific purpose in the treatment and prevention of cardiac issues.</p></p>
