<p><p>The 10G transformer market consists of two main types: PoE (Power over Ethernet) and Non-PoE. PoE transformers are designed to deliver power and data simultaneously through Ethernet cables, making them ideal for devices like IP cameras and wireless access points. On the other hand, Non-PoE transformers do not have this capability and are commonly used for data-only applications. Both types cater to different market needs and play a crucial role in enabling high-speed and reliable data transmission in various networking applications.</p></p>
