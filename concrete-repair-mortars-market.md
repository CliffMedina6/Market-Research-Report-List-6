<p><strong>The Concrete Repair Mortars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-modified Cementitious Mortar</li><li>Epoxy-based Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete repair mortars are commonly categorized into two types: Polymer-modified cementitious mortar and Epoxy-based mortar. Polymer-modified cementitious mortars are composed of cement, sand, polymers, and other additives to enhance adhesion, durability, and flexibility. They are suitable for overhead and vertical repair work. Epoxy-based mortars are made of epoxy resins and hardeners, providing high strength, chemical resistance, and low permeability. They are commonly used for repairing cracks, spalls, and damaged concrete surfaces in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
