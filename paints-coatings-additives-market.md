<p><p>The Paints & Coatings Additives market includes various types such as Biocides, Dispersants & Wetting Agents, Stabilizers, Defoamers, and Dearaters. Biocides are used to prevent microbial growth, while Dispersants & Wetting Agents aid in dispersing pigments evenly in the paint. Stabilizers help maintain the quality and durability of the paint, while Defoamers reduce foam formation during production. Dearaters help eliminate air bubbles in the paint. Each type of additive plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance and quality of paints and coatings.</p></p>
