<p><p>The riflescope market comprises various types tailored for specific shooting needs. Telescopic sights utilize lenses to magnify distant targets, enhancing accuracy for precision shooting. Collimating optical sights offer a parallax-free view, allowing fast target acquisition with minimal distortion, often used in tactical scenarios. Reflex sights provide a reticle projected onto a lens, enabling quick aiming while keeping both eyes open, ideal for close-quarters engagements. Each type serves distinct shooting styles, contributing to the diverse landscape of riflescope options available.</p></p>
