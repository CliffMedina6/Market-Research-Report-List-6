<p><p>Venipuncture needles are classified into two main types based on their flexibility: soft needles and rigid needles. Soft needles are made of materials like plastic or silicone, which are more flexible and bendable, providing a gentler insertion and reducing the risk of vein damage or patient discomfort. On the other hand, rigid needles are made of materials like stainless steel, which are more sturdy and less flexible, allowing for easier penetration of tough skin or hard-to-reach veins. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for venipuncture needles.</p></p>
