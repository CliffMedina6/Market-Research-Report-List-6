<p><p>The Visual Modular Holding Cabinet market is categorized into two main types: Bottom Heat and Top and Bottom Heat. Bottom Heat cabinets primarily provide heat from the bottom, ensuring food remains warm without drying out. In contrast, Top and Bottom Heat cabinets offer heat from both the top and bottom, allowing for more uniform temperature distribution and optimal food preservation. These varying heating methods cater to different needs in commercial kitchens, enhancing the efficiency of food service operations.</p></p>
