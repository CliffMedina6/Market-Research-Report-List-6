<p><strong>The Single Break Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Break Isolator market is segmented into three primary types: Bus Side, Line Side, and Transfer Bus Side. </p><p>Bus Side isolators are installed on the busbar system to ensure safe maintenance without interrupting the utility supply. </p><p>Line Side isolators connect the incoming power supply to the load, isolating it during maintenance. </p><p>Transfer Bus Side isolators allow for the switching of loads between two busbars, enhancing system reliability. Each type serves a vital role in electrical safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
