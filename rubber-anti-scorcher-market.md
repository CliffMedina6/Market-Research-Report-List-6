<p><p>The Rubber Anti Scorcher market is divided into two primary types: powder and granular. Powder anti scorch agents are finely milled to ensure uniform dispersion in rubber formulations, enhancing processing stability and preventing premature curing. Granular anti scorch agents, on the other hand, are coarser, offering advantages in handling and mixing, often providing controlled release properties during the vulcanization process. Both forms cater to specific applications within the rubber industry, optimizing performance based on processing requirements and end-use characteristics.</p></p>
