<p><p>The wide format scanner market includes two primary types: large flatbed scanners and feed-through scanners. Large flatbed scanners offer versatility for scanning oversized documents, artwork, and maps with high-resolution quality on a flat surface, making them ideal for detailed archival work. In contrast, feed-through scanners are designed for efficient scanning of multiple documents in a continuous feed, enabling high-speed processing for everyday office tasks. Both types cater to varying needs across industries such as architecture, engineering, and graphic design.</p></p>
